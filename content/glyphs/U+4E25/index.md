Shortening of [嚴](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/30/#U%2b56B4) based on the cursive form of the upper part with deleting the inner element. Modern simplified form of [嚴](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/30/#U%2b56B4) in China.
