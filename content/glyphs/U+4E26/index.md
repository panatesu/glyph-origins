{並} \*bˤaŋʔ "to stand side by side" → {併} \*bˤeŋʔ "to stand side by
side; together"

Depiction of two people standing side by side. Later a synonymous word
{併} became the main one for this glyph.

- 季旭昇 2014 - 說文新證 \[2nd ed.\] (783-784)
- 裘錫圭 2002 - 讀書札記四則・“秉時養人”之“秉”爲“並”之音近訛字
- 布之道 2022 - 廣韻形聲考
