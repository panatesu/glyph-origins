?{㘈} \*\[ŋ\](r)ək "smart child" ♪→ {疑} \*\[ŋ\](r)ə "to doubt"

Initally the word {疑} was written as 𠤗 (depiction of a person with a
cane (\> 止) looking around); sometimes 彳 *ROAD* and ♪牛 \*ŊᵂƏ were
added. The modern glyph comes either from 子 *CHILD* + ♪𠤗 \*ŊƏ for the
word {㘈} or from the distortion 牛 \> 子.
