{(菩)薩} (*bu)sat* "bodhisattva"

Assimilated form of 薛 from 薛埵 \*siat.tuɑiᴮ "sattva" via adding 土
from 埵 to the bottom right. The appearence of 生 "to live" (\> 產) in
the bottom right part is probably influenced by Sanskrit *sat*
"existing; living".

- 岩松浅夫 1991 - 「薩」字の成立とその變遷
- 張涌泉 2010 - 漢語俗字研究 \[2nd ed.\] (180)
