#### 示¹

{祇} \*ɡe "earth spirit"

A differentiated form of [主](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/3/#U%2b4E3B) (depiction of an ancestral tablet).

- 方稚松 2009 - 殷墟甲骨文五種記事刻辭研究
- 補骨脂 2025 - 重論“示”諧聲域——從清華簡《胥馬》“⿰骨旨”字說起
- 季旭昇 2014 - 說文新證 \[2nd ed.\] (48-49)

#### 示²

{示} \*giᶜ ~ \*dźiᶜ "to show"

Shortened form of [視](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/113/#U%2b8996). The modern pronunciation is based on a dialectal Han pronunciation which underwent palatalization.

- 補骨脂 2025 - 重論“示”諧聲域——從清華簡《胥馬》“⿰骨旨”字說起
