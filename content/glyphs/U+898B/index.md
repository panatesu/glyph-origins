{見} \*kˤens "to see"

Depiction of a kneeling person (卩) with the emphasis on the eye (目).

- 季旭昇 2014 - 說文新證 \[2nd ed.\] (694)

**Forms:**

见 - Shortening based on the cursive form. Modern simplified form in
China.
