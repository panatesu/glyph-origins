{左} \*tsˤa\[j\]ʔ "left"

Initially the word {左} was written as [𠂇](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/4/#U%2b20087) (depiction of a left hand). Later [工](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/48/#U%2b5DE5) was added (function unknown, likely a decorative element).
