{噎} \*qˤi\[k\] "to choke" ♪→ {一/壹} \*ʔit "one"

Shortened form of 㱅 (depiction of a person with something stuck in his
mouth for {噎}). Later ♪吉 \*KIT was added.

- 蔣玉斌 2013 - 甲骨綴合所得新字新形研究

**Forms:**

壱 - Shortening based on the cursive form. Modern simplified form in
Japan.
