#### 安¹

![](https://i.postimg.cc/ZK2KKHL0/U-5B89.png)

{安} \*ʔˤan "to sit"

Initially the word {安} was written as 𱙁 or ⿱卩一 (depiction of a
kneeling person (女/卩) with a line under the buttocks, either conveying
the idea of sitting or representing a mat). Later 宀 *ROOM* was added.

- 陳劍 2005 - 說“安”字
- 季旭昇 2014 - 說文新證 \[2nd ed.\] (592-593)
