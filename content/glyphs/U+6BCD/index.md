{母} \*mˤəʔ "mother" & {女} \*nraʔ "woman"

[女](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/38/#U%2b5973) + differentiative dots. Initially, like [女](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/38/#U%2b5973), it was used for both words {女} and {母}. Later it began to be used only for {母}.
