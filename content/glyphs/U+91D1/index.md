{金} \*k(r)um "bronze"

Initially the word {金} was written as 火 *FIRE +* ♪今 \*KUM. Later 呂
*METAL* was added.

- 黃天樹 2005 - 花園莊東地甲骨中所見的若干新資料
- 季旭昇 2014 - 說文新證 \[2nd ed.\] (925-926)

**Forms:**

钅- Shortening based on the cursive form in the left part of compound
glyphs. Modern simplified form in China.
