{小} \*\[s\]ewʔ "small" & {少} \*\[s.t\]ewʔ "few" & {杪/藐} \*\[m\]ewʔ "small"

Abstract depiction of three small objects. Initially it was used for words {小}, {少} and {杪/藐} (or for one of these word, if the reconstruction *MEW for all of them turns out to be true). Later it began to be used only for {小}.
