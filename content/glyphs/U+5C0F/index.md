{小} \*sewʔ "small" & {少} \*s.tewʔ "few" & {杪/藐} \*mewʔ "small"

Abstract depiction of three small objects. Initially it was used for
words {小}, {少} and {杪/藐}. Later it began to be used only for {小}.
