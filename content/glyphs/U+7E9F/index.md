Shortening of [糸](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/120/#U%2b7CF8) based on the cursive form in the left part of compound glyphs. Modern simplified form of [糸](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/120/#U%2b7CF8) in China.
