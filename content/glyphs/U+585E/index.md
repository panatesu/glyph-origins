{塞} \*\[s\]ˤək "to block up" or {塞} \*\[s\]ˤəks "fortress"

Initially the word {塞} was written as 𡫳 (depiction of hands (廾)
placing pillars (㠭) inside a building (宀)). Later 土 *EARTH* was
added.

- 于省吾 1979 - 甲骨文字釋林・釋𡫳
