{餼} \*qʰ(r)ə\[t\]s "to present grain" ♪→ {氣} \*C.qʰə\[t\]s "air"

[米](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/119/#U%2b7C73) *RICE* + ♪[气](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/84/#U%2b6C14) \*QƏ\[T\].

- 林志強 & al. (ed.) 2017 - 《文源》評注 (607)

**Forms:**

[気](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/84/#U%2b6C17) - Shortening based on the replacement of 米 with 㐅. Modern simplified form in Japan.

[气](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/84/#U%2b6C14) - Preservation of the ancient glyph for {氣}. Modern simplified form in China.
