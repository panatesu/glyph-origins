{弦} \*ɡˤin "bowstring"

Depiction of a bow (弓) with a pointer on the bowstring. Later the
bowstring was changed into 糸 *STRING*, and 弓 *BOW* was added. Then the
right element was changed into ♪玄 \*KIN.
