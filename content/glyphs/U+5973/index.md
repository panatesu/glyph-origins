?{如} \*na "to obey" ♪→ {女} \*nraʔ "woman" & {母} \*mˤəʔ "mother"

Depiction of a kneeling person with crossed arms. Later a decorative
line was added. Initially it was used for both words {女} and {母}.
Later after the glyph 母 was created, 女 began to be used only for {女}.
