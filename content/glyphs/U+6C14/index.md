?{氣} \*C.qʰə\[t\]s "air"

Likely a shortened form of [⿰奚戌](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/215/#%E2%BF%B0%E5%A5%9A%E6%88%8C) \*QƏ\[T\] (depiction of decapitating a tied slave for {刏} \*\[q\]ʷˤə\[t\]s "to cut off").

Modern simplified form of [氣](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/84/#U%2b6C23) in China.
