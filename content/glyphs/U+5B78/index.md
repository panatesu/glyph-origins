{學} \*m.kˤruk "to study"

Initially the word {學} was written with various combinations of 宀
*ROOM,* 𦥑 *CULTIVATE* and ♪爻 \*KUK. Later 子 *CHILD* was added.

- 季旭昇 2014 - 說文新證 \[2nd ed.\] (247-248)

**Forms:**

学 - Shortening based on the cursive form. Modern simplified form in
China and Japan.
