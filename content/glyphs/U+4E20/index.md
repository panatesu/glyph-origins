#### 丠¹

Copy of the small seal script form of [丘](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/1/#U%2b4E18) from *Shuowen Jiezi*.

#### 丠²

Shortening of [冀](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/12/#U%2b5180) replacing 異 *YI* with homophonous 一 *YI*. Was included in the second round of simplification for [冀](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/12/#U%2b5180).

- Bökset R. 2021 - Long Story of Short Forms: Simplified Chinese Characters from A to Z (146-147)
