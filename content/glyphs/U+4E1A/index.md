#### 业¹

Shortening of [業](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/75/#U%2b696D). Modern simplified form of [業](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/75/#U%2b696D) in China.

- Bökset R. 2021 - Long Story of Short Forms: Simplified Chinese Characters from A to Z (338)

#### 业²

Copy of the small seal script form of [丘](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/1/#U%2b4E18) from *Shuowen Jiezi*.

#### 业³

Alternative form of [北](https://panatesu.github.io/glyph-origins/radicals/21/#U%2b5317).
