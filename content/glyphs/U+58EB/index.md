{士} \*\[dz\]rəʔ "knight" & {王} \*waŋ "king"

Depiction of a downward-facing axe blade as a symbol of power. Initially
it was used for both words {王} and {士}. Later it began to be used only
for {士}.

- 林澐 1965 - 說王
- 季旭昇 2014 - 說文新證 \[2nd ed.\] (59-50)
