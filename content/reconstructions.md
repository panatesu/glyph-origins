The system presented here is a compromise between several
reconstructions. The main one being:

- Baxter W.H. & Sagart L. 2014 - Old Chinese. A New Reconstruction

It's slightly changed in some respects given the following
reconstruction utilizing modern palaeographical research:

- 布之道 2022 - 廣韻形聲考

Other than that, some changes are made when the choise of a
reconstruction is not wise in Baxter & Sagart 2014 (the notable example
being 火). Changes include research on other Trans-Himalayan branches
(especially Gyalrongic), palaeographical material, modern Sinitic
topolects, other (S)EA languages etc. Some of the resources utilized:

- Старостин С.А. 1989 - Реконструкция древнекитайской фонологической
  системы
- Baxter W.H. 1992 - A Handbook of Old Chinese Phonology

- Schuessler A. 2009 - Minimal Old Chinese and Later Han Chinese

  sesquisyllabic phonology
- Hill N.W. 2019 - The Historical Phonology of Tibetan, Burmese, and
  Chinese
- Zhang S. 章舒婭 & al. 2019 - A study of cognates between Gyalrong
  languages and Old Chinese
- Jacques G. 2020 - Voiced obstruents in Mien and Old Chinese
  reconstrucions
